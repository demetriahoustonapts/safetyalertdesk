What’s happening: Credit card fraud continues to affect consumers nationwide, with many victims unaware until weeks later. Small unauthorized charges are often used to test stolen card numbers.

Scammers may begin with charges under $10 to avoid detection before making larger purchases.

AdSense Ad Slot

Who may be affected

Any cardholder can be targeted, including those who regularly shop online, use public Wi-Fi, or store card information on websites.

Warning signs to watch for

  • Small unfamiliar charges
  • Multiple pending transactions
  • Declined purchases without explanation

What to do immediately

  • Review statements regularly
  • Report suspicious charges to your bank
  • Freeze or replace the card if needed
  • Monitor future activity closely

Early reporting limits financial damage and speeds up resolution.
