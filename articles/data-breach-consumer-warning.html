What’s happening: Data breaches continue to affect companies across retail, healthcare, and online services, exposing customer information.

Even when companies act quickly, consumers may still face risks long after a breach occurs.

Information commonly exposed

  • Email addresses
  • Passwords
  • Phone numbers
  • Account details

Steps you should take

  • Change passwords immediately
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Monitor bank and credit accounts
  • Be alert for phishing attempts

Why vigilance matters

Stolen information is often reused months later. Staying proactive reduces long-term risk.
