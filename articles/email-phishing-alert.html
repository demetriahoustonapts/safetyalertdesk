Email Phishing Alert: How to Spot Dangerous Messages

Email scams · Updated recently

Phishing emails often appear to come from trusted companies and ask you to click links or download attachments that steal information.

Red flags

  • Unexpected account warnings
  • Misspellings or unusual sender addresses
  • Links that don’t match official websites

Protection tips

  • Don’t click suspicious links
  • Verify messages directly with companies
  • Use spam filters and security software
