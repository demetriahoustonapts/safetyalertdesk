What’s happening: Consumers are reporting scam calls and messages claiming overdue utility bills and threatening immediate service shutoff.

Common warning signs

  • Urgent payment demands
  • Requests for gift cards or prepaid payments
  • Caller pressure to act immediately

What to do

  • Contact your utility company directly
  • Do not share payment information
  • Report suspicious calls
