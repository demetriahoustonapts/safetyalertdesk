Utility Bill Scam Alert: Fake Disconnect Notices

Utility scams · Updated recently

Scammers impersonate utility companies claiming your power, gas, or water will be shut off unless you make immediate payment. These messages often arrive by phone, text, or email.

Common warning signs

  • Urgent payment demands
  • Requests for gift cards or wire transfers
  • Caller refuses to verify account details

What to do

  • Contact your utility provider using the number on your bill
  • Never pay utilities with gift cards
  • Report scams to your state utility commission
