Medical Billing Scam Alert: Fake Collection Calls

Healthcare scams · Updated recently

Scammers impersonate hospitals or collection agencies claiming unpaid medical bills. They pressure victims into immediate payment.

Red flags

  • Threats of arrest or lawsuits
  • Requests for gift cards or wire transfers
  • Refusal to send written documentation

What to do

  • Request written proof
  • Contact your provider directly
  • Check insurance explanations of benefits
