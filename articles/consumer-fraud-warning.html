Consumer Fraud Warning: Common Tactics to Watch For

Fraud prevention · Updated recently

Consumer fraud takes many forms, including impersonation scams, fake invoices, investment fraud, and online marketplace deception.

Common tactics

  • Urgency and fear-based messaging
  • Requests for secrecy
  • Unusual payment methods

Staying protected

  • Verify before paying or sharing information
  • Pause before acting on urgent requests
  • Report fraud attempts to authorities
