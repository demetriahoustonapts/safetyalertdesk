Job Offer Scam Warning: Fake Recruiters and Remote Traps

Employment scams · Updated recently

Scammers pose as recruiters offering high-paying remote jobs. Victims are often asked to pay fees, deposit fake checks, or share personal information.

Warning signs

  • Job offers without interviews
  • Requests to pay onboarding or equipment fees
  • Communication only via text or messaging apps
  • Urgent pressure to accept

How to protect yourself

  • Research the company independently
  • Never pay to get a job
  • Verify recruiters through official websites
