What’s happening: Scammers are increasingly impersonating banks and credit unions through phone calls, emails, and text messages to obtain personal and financial information.

AdSense Ad Slot

Common warning signs

  • Urgent messages claiming suspicious activity
  • Requests to verify account details
  • Caller ID spoofing real bank numbers

What consumers should do

  • Hang up and contact your bank directly
  • Never share one-time passcodes
  • Report suspicious messages immediately

Legitimate banks will not pressure you to act immediately or request sensitive information unexpectedly.
